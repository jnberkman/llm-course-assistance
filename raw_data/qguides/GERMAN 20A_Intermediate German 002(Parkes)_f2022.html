Comments
20a is basically a continuation of the 10ab series, same text and everything. The readings get more complex and the cultural immersion part grows, as we spend more time looking at films and German texts
The content of German 20A is a big jump from beginning German. I find the grammar, vocabulary, reading, and writing assignments difficult. It assumes students already have a strong grasp of all the case endings, adjective endings, past participles, etc., which was not the case for me. I have mixed feelings about this class. On the one hand, my German did improve, so I have to give this class credit for that. Yet on the other hand, I also feel that what I learned was superficial because I was given so much material that I never had the chance to learn anything well.
