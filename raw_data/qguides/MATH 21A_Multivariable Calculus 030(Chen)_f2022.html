If you must take a math class at Harvard, take this one!! 100% choose it over Math1b or anything lower. You must be familiar with taking derivatives and integrals for this course (but not the extremely complex versions) and everything else will be taught to you! This is easily the best–taught math class I have ever been in. For context, I took this freshman fall. I had placed into the math classes a level above math 1b (math 18/19, 21, 22, etc), but I was strongly considering redoing math 1b thinking that I needed a stronger foundation in math no matter which field I decided (econ/stem concentrator). I am so glad that I chose to take math 21a instead of 1b. Talking to friends in 1b, it is a notoriously hard class that almost nobody enjoys. Math 21a is also a difficult course, but in contrast to 1b, I think that it is more manageable and (from people I have talked to who took both classes) better structured. If math is not your strength, taking 21a also gives you the advantage of taking one high–level class and being done with math for good, since it fulfills many concentration requirements for honors track or a high–level math course above 1b. Take this class if you have to take a math course. It will be work, but it's a very rewarding and fulfilling course.
