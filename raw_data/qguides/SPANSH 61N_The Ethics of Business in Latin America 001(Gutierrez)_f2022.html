You get what you put into the course and the analyses.
This class was really really incredible!!! If you can take it with Wilnomy, definitely do it. They are an incredible professor, they teach with so much passion and dedication, they are so interested in the subject matter and it really shows. This class is not so much about business as it is about the conditions of workers in various industries, which I found ultimately more interesting. Assignments are painless and actually fun to complete. Exams are not difficult either if you've done the reading and shown up to class! Overall, I improved my spanish by a lot and discussions with my classmates were really fruitful and stimulating.
Spanish 61 is structured around reading assigned materials and then discussing them in class. This means that in order to succeed in the course, it is important to be able to keep up with both the reading and the discussions. If you are able to do that, you will find this class to be incredibly engaging and rewarding. The instructor is knowledgeable and provides valuable insights during discussions, and the class is filled with passionate and curious students.
Amazing!!!!!
