What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Comments
German 10A is honestly a super fun class and you do learn a good amount of German during your first semester. Wilhelm makes the 9 am classes worth it, so take it with him if you can. From taking this class you'll fall in love with the language and its quirks. Learning German is useful if you ever want to spend time abroad, and you'll find that it pops up a lot in the news and in art and culture.
Take it if you want to learn German.
The German department and all the teachers are excellent. They are really committed to your success and will do everything possible to ensure you do well. While the daily homework assignments can be tedious, they are really helpful for your overall success in the course.
If you are interested in learning German, you absolutely should. It's a department that is interested in getting you excited about Germany. There are many cultural events and opportunities and the professors are great. Like any language class, it requires studying and is easy to fall behind in. But if you like learning languages or you have to take a language anyway for your requirement, this is a great one to take.
