If you're debating between two languages to take, or if you're considering taking a higher level language course for a language you already took in high school, I highly suggest you choose French 10. It's a very worthwhile course if you're willing to put in the enthusiasm and effort to learn a new language (and it's miles better than trying to learn French off of Duolingo!).
It can be overwhelming at first, but stick with it! Aim to practice a little every day (already built in with homework). Try to think in French, too!
Great class, but be ready to put in the work. Memorizing vocabulary and grammar tactics, and practicing speaking on your own are key to succeeding in this class.
This is a time consuming class, but you will feel your progress, which is gratifying.
