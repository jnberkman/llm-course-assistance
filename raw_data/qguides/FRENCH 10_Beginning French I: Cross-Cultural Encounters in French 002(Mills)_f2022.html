Be prepared for a fast paced experience that can become really rewarding if you spend a good deal of time practicing. It is an easy class to enjoy when you put forward a good effort.
Please don't take french because you want to impress your crush or anyone else. Take it because you are genuinely curious about the language and are prepared to fully immerse yourself in it to do well. French 10 is an amazing class and I do not regret taking it but I wished I had a genuine passion for the class.
It's a great class. It moves at a quick pace, and having never taken French before this, I didn't always feel like I was caught up, especially at the beginning of the semester. But you catch on as it goes and figure out the best way for you to study and learn the material. I feel like I've learned a lot of French and a lot about the culture in just a few short months.
They should learn class materials every day and not procrastinate
Do not take this course. It is not worth it. Although a 94+ is an A, the TF told us that they are not allowed to give students above a 98 on any of the subcategories, except the quizzes. On essays, the teachers will always find something that is incorrect, even after you have made all the edits they tell you to make for the different drafts. This creates unnecessary stress on you. Also, many of the students in the class have already taken some level of French, so if this is your first time, good luck. We are constantly told to not take risk, which prevents us from getting better. The information is present in such confusing ways, leaving us confused about what to study for quizzes and exams. The grammar is never explained clearly, which means that a lot of the information will be self–taught. Also, for the essays, you will have one due the third week of school and will be marked off points for each and every mistake. This can become scary because if this is your first time taking French, you are discouraged from taking risks on your essays and truly learn in fear of making a mistake.
If you plan to take this class go to office hours and use the resources provided, do not just do the homework! This course goes very fast so if you don't put effort in every week the course will continue to get harder. Don't take this class if you just want an easy A.
If you are really enthusiastic about taking French (or you need the credit) I recommend taking it, otherwise, it can be a bit time consuming with class and homework everyday. But overall you will learn a lot. By now, I can have a full conversation with someone and somewhat be able to get around in a French speaking country!
If you are interested in French it's a decent class, but it's a significant volume of work and there are probably easier classes for the requirement if that's all you care about.
Good class if you have some previous experience with the language, if not it will probably be pretty time–consuming.
