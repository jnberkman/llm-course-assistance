poorly taught class that was beyond complication. things could have been taught much simpler which would have resulted in us better understanding the content. instead, we focused on the math which confused everyone. STAY AWAY! choose another tutorial topic! you'll thank me.
The biggest improvement would be using Canvas. I don't understand how a young teacher like Ross refuses to use a program as simple as Canvas, it can't be that hard. Having to use email for every aspect of the class was so much more complicated than it needed to be. Ross would email us the night before class to give us the readings for the next day and tell us there was an assignment due too.
In addition to using Canvas, I think grading assignments is a pretty basic requirement of teaching. Ross graded three of our assignments the whole year out of maybe 12, and even then, he just sent us an email with our grade. We were left completely in the dark the whole year about where our grades stood.
I also think the pset portion of the class was mishandled. Every other tutorial used the same psets so people could go to the general Stata/R office hours and get help. Instead, Ross created his own psets that were much harder and could only be done in R. Because the psets were different, this kept us from being able to utilize the 970 office hours.
Ec 970 is a difficult class, with many readings and written assignments. Unfortunately, it is a department requirement, so make sure to have taken econometrics beforehand, and that you are at least somewhat interested in your section's topic.
