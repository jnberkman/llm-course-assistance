This biggest reservation I would have about this class is that the content matter is super boring. There's a chance you have to take it because its your level, you want some practice, etc. but if at all possible, look at the topic matter of French 20 and 40 and decide if you could maybe do one of those instead. The focus of the class was very boring and I often found myself just wanting to get through the class period. The projects weren't too bad at all: I have friends in other language courses who were always doing the most annoying and time–intensive projects, however this class was quite manageable. The homework isn't bad AT ALL either. The final is a debate, which is super easy.
This was a good French class. It was chill.
