Take this class! It is a fun way to learn spanish. Classes are pretty relaxed and we mostly discuss the text that we read with some grammar lessons. The overall assignments are fun and creative, they do not feel like exams.
The class was great! A little intimidating if you are not a performer, but it is definitely manageable and even fun. If you can, take the class with Cristina. She was an amazing section leader!
TAKE THIS COURSE!!! This course was the highlight of my semester, and I could not say enough good things about this course and Cristina. Cristina was the shining highlight of this course and I could go on para siempre about how she has helped me grow as a person and as a Spanish speaker. The course and Cristina were incredibly helpful in improving my speaking skills and pronunciation, and the compositions were always engaging. There was never a second where I felt like this course was not making me a better speaker. Cristina also was an amazing instructor. She truly has a knack for teaching the language on top of being one of the coolest and sweetest human beings at Harvard. Even during a rough patch, Cristina offered support and additional help. She truly is an above and beyond instructor! In short, TAKE THIS COURSE!!
This class is a great class! All exams are oral exams (performances of monologues and dialogues that you write) with a good amount of leniency for memorization and grammar as long as what you are saying is complex and demonstrates a depth of understanding of the related readings. It is pretty manageable with an assignment due every other week and exams spread out.
Spanish 50 is an excellent course! The readings are really interesting, and you will really grow as a speaker and a listener from taking this course. The acting component will help you learn Spanish, but it's also just a lot of fun! You should take Spanish 50!
This is a great class for anyone who wants to improve their written and spoken Spanish skills. You will get out what you put into this course.
