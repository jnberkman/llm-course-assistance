Take this class! This is almost without a doubt the best way to fulfill the language requirement, especially if it's taught by Carmen, who was our instructor and was just wonderful. As long as you do the work and try your best to learn the material, it'll be a good time. The class is designed to help you succeed. The biweekly quizzes are a good way to keep you accountable with the grammar and vocab that you're learning without being overly stressful or difficulty, and the projects are usually pretty fun.
Take this class! Especially if Profa Carmen is teaching!
Spanish 15 was an amazing course with an awesome instructor! Profa Carmen truly cares about the success of each and every one of her students and she is so dedicated to ensuring that the class is a comfortable learning environment. The fast–paced nature of this course makes it ideal for learning a new language. The daily assignments are not too bad and as long as you show a solid effort on projects, you will likely receive a good grade. The class makes grades something not to be too stressed about, which makes this class even more enjoyable. Overall, if you have even the slightest interest in learning Spanish, I would highly recommend this course!
I learned so much from this course! It was a really great way to immerse myself in Spanish for a semester, and was probably more helpful than multiple years of a high school foreign language course. Be aware that you will do better in this class if you can dedicate lots of time to it outside of class (i.e. at least 1 hour per day, preferably more).
Friends, colleagues, please please take this class. It may be one of the best decisions that I ever made in my life at Harvard. I came in with zero, I say ZEEERRROOO Spanish background, and by the end of the semester, I was speaking quite fluently with my classmates, friends, and even during my travels to South America. It may be a fast–moving class but you will grow to like the speed, and you will learn a LOT of Spanish in a short time while learning a lot about Latin–America from authentic sources.
Also, Dr. Carmen Teresa Fajardo Rojas should be rated one of the best people in this world if not the absolute best. She taught this class with so much passion and it was actually her very first time in the US. She made our class to be very tight–knit; she was very funny, creative, and always gave a reassuring smile. She was very encouraging and understanding and genuinely cared for each of us. By the end of the semester I made a long conversation with her in Spanish, just getting to know more about each other, and I was surprised that I could actually do that yet 14 weeks before I only knew the word "Holla". Yes, please, por favor, take this class with her.
The class schedule of 5 days a week at 9am can be rough at times but I think it was worth it! I grew so much in my Spanish language skills and learned a lot in one semester. The class never felt overwhelming, it was well paced. The other student in the class were all kind and we had a great teaching assistant so you will be well supported.
I cannot recommend Spanish 15 enough! The benefits are endless: complete the language requirement, make new friends, have something fun to look forward to each morning, boost your GPA, and, of course, become very very proficient with Spanish (you will surprise yourself with how good you get)!
TAKE THIS CLASS! The professor is very kind and understanding. It is a great way to get the language requirement out of the way in one semester. The class goes fast, but I highly recommend the course!
CARMEN IS THE BEST!!! I hope you get her as your TF, you will learn so much. Make sure to ask lots of questions and never be afraid to speak in class
