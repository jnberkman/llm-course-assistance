This class is excellent! If you already know a bit of Spanish and want to learn Portuguese, take this class. The languages are similar, but this class tailors the language toward those with a Spanish background and even explicitly notes specific differences on occasion. With Marcia as our instructor, my class had a great experience and bonded over learning a new language, which made class discussions both productive and fun. This was definitely one of my favorite courses I've taken at Harvard!
Great course! Don't doubt enrolling!

Marcia is a fun and engaging teacher, she will keep you excited about learning a new language and in a a fun way!
The work for this class is extremely doable, and by the end of the semester, it's clear that we'd learned so much!! Marcia is an amazing teacher, and she created a space where I felt comfortable making mistakes and learning from them.
If you're thinking of taking this course because you want to learn Portuguese, think no further. This is an absolutely fantastic introductory course that will make you engage with a wide variety of materials concerning the Portuguese language and cultures. The teacher, Marcia, is the best language instructor I have had in a while –– she will welcome you with open arms and pão de queijo and take you by the hand as you navigate the intricacies of the language. Cannot recommend enough, you'll be back for 11S next semester!
