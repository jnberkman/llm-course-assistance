I think this is definitely one of the best sophomore tutorials you can take. It is a rigorous course and has lots of assignments, but overall the topics were interesting and class discussions were insightful. This is a very very difficult class. Choose a topic that is interesting to you, otherwise it will be miserable. Schedule your course map around this (take easy classes alongside it). Good luck
This was the best economics course I’ve taken. The course begins with a lot of readings on fish.. I’ll admit, when I think of natural resources , I think of oil and gas and a lot of other things before I come around to fish. However, Andres chose only the coolest economics research on fish (and other natural resource topics) for this course. Moreover, he does a great job at making often confusing, complicated economics concepts seem intuitive. Would definitely recommend this tutorial to a friend.
