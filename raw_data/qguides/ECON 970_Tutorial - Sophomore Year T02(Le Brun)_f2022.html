What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Comments
Take. This. Class. It is the best class I've taken at Harvard (yes, best), and I cannot recommend this enough. It's brilliant, helpful, fun, and just so so relevant. There is only one assignment per week except for some weeks where there are none, but they are all relatively easy, don't take too much time, and are 12/10 interesting. Coming from someone who hates both reading and writing, every reading and writing assignment in this class was enjoyable and didn't take long. Set aside 1 hour for class readings (30 minutes before each class) and 2–3 hours for assignments and that's all! Coding p–sets may be a little more challenging and take a bit more time if you have no experience in Stata or R, but literally everything is provided for you. I had no experience in Stata and all I did was follow the guides and did perfectly fine! For my final paper, when I did my first "by myself for myself" coding (with no help from guides or without any assignments telling me what to do), it was the most satisfying thing I'd ever experienced. Coding assignments and 2–3 of the later assignments that were long took a bit longer to finish, but they were still enjoyable as well. Plus, remember that there are office hours that you can go to if you're confused. Also, the instructor, Ronak Jain, is INCREDIBLE, so please reach out to her for literally anything. She WILL be helpful.
Ronak is an incredible TF! Please take her tutorial if you get it. She makes the class fun and interesting and is very accessible and grades very fairly.
