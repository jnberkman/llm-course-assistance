Overall, a solid course. The workload is HEAVY heavy with 3 P–sets a week... This was my only P–set class but it was still the bane of my existence a lot of the time. For me, the course was most difficult in the beginning because I wasn't accustomed to the way limits are taught here (I'm an international). But I improved exponentially just by virtue of doing SO much math practice every week via P–sets. And eventually we moved back into more familiar territory (ie some parts of differentiation and integration), making the latter half of the course a lot easier on me. So if you end up struggling a little in the beginning, I'd say don't give up! But consider carefully whether you are willing/able to do three P–sets a week before enrolling in Math 1A.
