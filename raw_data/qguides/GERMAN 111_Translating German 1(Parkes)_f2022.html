Comments
Take this course if you are interested in translation! It has a great emphasis of learning about the fundamental issues that arise when translating German texts, and then, it provides practical exercises to test your understanding of the material! The professor is also really great and makes the classroom a great and interactive learning environment.
If you enjoy German, TAKE THIS CLASS! Professor Parkes creates a welcoming, non–judgmental environment and emphasizes learning/ personal growth. She minimizes grade–related stress and just encourages you to do your best and learn from your mistakes (she provides extensive feedback on assignments). I went from zero translation knowledge to having an entire translation strategies toolkit at my disposal. HIGHLY RECOMMEND! I loved this class.
