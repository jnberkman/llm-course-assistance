What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Table for [QTitle].
Comments
Take this class! It was my favorite course this semester!
I highly recommend this course; it was probably my favorite class I took this semester! Spanish 40's focus is primarily on watching movies from Spanish–speaking countries and analyzing them while learning grammar throughout. You will be forced to speak Spanish a lot during class (this may seem scary but you'll get used to it fairly quickly) and that is how you improve your speaking abilities. You'll also pick up on the reading and writing as you will read many short stories and write several essays. You do get chances to revise your essays, which is another way to boost your grade. The quizzes are fairly easy and the final project (no final exam) is fun to make, though make sure to not film your scenes the day before its due. Shoutout to el profe Ricardo!!
I would tell future students to please watch the movies and do the readings as soon as possible; get them done and out of the way and give them a glance before class so that you can discuss but don't let them be the last thing you do. Also, do the compositions as soon as possible as well, you have the opportunity to correct them and resubmit and do better! If you place into Spanish 30, you can also take this class – it's totally feasible.
