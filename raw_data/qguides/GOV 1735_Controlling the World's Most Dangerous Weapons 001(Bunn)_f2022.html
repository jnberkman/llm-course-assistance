I recommend this class if you want to engage in policy memos, simulations and participation in a class with mostly graduate students. It certainly pushed me out of my comfort zone and I learned a lot!
This class is one of the very BEST I have ever taken!!!! Even if you are not particularly passionate about being a nuclear weapons expert, this class is still a one–of–its–kind experience and will make you a better thinker. Every Tuesday and Thursday morning at 9am I dash to HKS with excitement for a fascinating lecture, and the assignments – which include two simulations – are challenging but super fun if you engage with them. Plus the classmates, who are mostly HKS students, will teach you a lot too.
Take it, and come to classes with an open mind!
Great course, engaging and experiencing prof, incredibly important topic!
This course is excellent! As an eminent expert who has done so much work in the field, Professor Bunn offers a truly unique peek behind the curtain on nuclear negotiations and arms control. This class was a truly unique experience, and possibly my favorite I have taken at Harvard so far. Be warned, if you don't already have a solid foundation in international relations/don't have a good intuition for how countries tend to respond to different international affairs and events, the policy memos and simulation assignments will be challenging. It is doable, but it can be stressful. Use think tank pieces if you don't know where to start for those!
